African Leopard

This animal is the African Leopard. They are seen most in the sub-Saharan Africa area and are known for their
camouflauge and stealth abilities. They also have great climbing skills to be able to get to their prey.

African Lion

This animal is the African Lion. They are seen most in the eastern and southern Africa area and are known
for being at the top of the food chain being the top predator in Africa. They are known for being the biggest cats and
the most fierce hunters in Africa.

African Elephant

This animal is the African Elephant. The elephant is the largest mammal on earth and are usually seen in the savannas
and forests in Africa. They are know for there distinct features such as their tusks and big ears.

Mountain Gorilla

This animal is the Mountain Gorilla. They are known for being very big and having immense strength. They are
usually seen in the forests of central Africa but are known to be dificult to spot.

Rhinoceros

This animal is the Rhinoceros. They are known for being a very powerful animal with their distinct feature of horns.
They are also herbivores and have poor eyesight but have a great sense of hearing and smell to compensate.
