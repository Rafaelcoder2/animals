Mountain Gorilla Details

The Mountain Gorilla's are known for being powerful but also gentle giants with incredible strength.
These endangered primates are known for being in the forests of central Africa but are difficult to track
because of their shy nature and their remote habitats. The mountain gorilla are known as well to have strong and close family bonds
especially when it comes to mating, where the male gorilla will protect the female gorilla and their family with their life.
The silverback, which is considered to be the alpha male protect the group and are the dominant leaders of their group as well.
